What is Randomness?

You may be used to thinking of the stochastic parts of random variables as ‘just chance’. In very select situations this is fine; radioactive decay really does appear to be ‘just chance’ (but ask Caffo about this...)

However, this is not what ‘random variables’ actually represent in most applications, and it can be a misleading simpliﬁcation to think that it’s ‘just chance’ that prevents us knowing the truth.
